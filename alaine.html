Our Story

A Taste of Heritage

Buko de Kalamay was born from a family tradition of kalamay-making in Bulacan. But it is actually rare to see Kalamay vendors here in our area. We were inspired to start a Kalamay business because it is a tasty traditional treat that many people love and accessed.

Today, we honor the Filipino delicacy of our favorite Kalama so we can also share it to each and everyone in Balingasag Misamis Oriental!

The Kalamay Tradition

Kalamay is a cherished Filipino delicacy made from glutinous rice flour, coconut milk, and muscovado sugar. The painstaking process of slow-cooking and constant stirring results in a uniquely sticky, sweet treat that's beloved across the islands.

Our version incorporates fresh buko (young coconut) for added texture and flavor, making it distinct from commercial varieties.

Our Traditional Process

1

Harvest

Fresh coconuts are harvested at peak ripeness from local farms in Quezon province.

2

Preparation

Glutinous rice is soaked overnight, then ground with fresh coconut milk.

3

Cooking

The mixture is slow-cooked in traditional kawali (woks) for 4-6 hours with constant stirring.

4

Packaging

Hand-wrapped in banana leaves or eco-friendly packaging for authentic presentation.

Quality Ingredients

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does your kalamay last? +

Our banana leaf-wrapped kalamay stays fresh for 5-7 days at room temperature and up to 2 weeks when refrigerated. Our commercial packaging extends shelf life to 3 weeks under refrigeration.

Do you offer nationwide shipping? +

Yes! We ship across the Philippines via LBC and J&T Express with specialized packaging to maintain freshness. Provincial orders may take 2-3 days for delivery.

Are your products vegan-friendly? +

All our kalamay products are 100% plant-based, using only coconut milk as dairy substitute. We never use animal products or by-products in our recipes.

Can I customize orders for special occasions? +

Absolutely! We offer custom packaging for birthdays, weddings, and corporate events with 2 weeks advance notice. Minimum order of 20 packs applies for customization.
